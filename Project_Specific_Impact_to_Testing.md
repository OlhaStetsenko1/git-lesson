### Project Specific Impact to Testing

The project aims to develop and launch a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for an e-commerce platform that allows users to browse, purchase, and manage orders for various products. The MVP will focus on core functionalities to provide a seamless user experience and validate the business model with early adopters.

#### Project Phase
- Minimum Viable Product (MVP)

#### Technology Stack
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express
- Database: MongoDB
- Hosting: AWS

#### Constraints & Assumptions
- Limited time frame for MVP development and testing
- Focus on core functionalities only
- Limited budget for testing resources

#### Critical Success Factors
- Seamless user experience
- Robust and secure payment processing
- Efficient order management system
- Positive feedback from early adopters

#### Time Zones
- Development and testing teams are distributed across different time zones (EST, PST, IST)
