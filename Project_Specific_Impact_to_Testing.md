### Project Specific Impact to Testing

The project aims to develop and launch a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for an e-commerce platform that allows users to browse, purchase, and manage orders for various products. The MVP will focus on core functionalities to provide a seamless user experience and validate the business model with early adopters.

#### Project Phase
The current phase of the project is the development and launch of the MVP. This phase will prioritize the implementation and testing of core functionalities essential for the e-commerce platform.

#### Technology Stack
The technology stack for this project includes:
- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: Node.js with Express.js
- Database: MongoDB
- Hosting: AWS

#### Constraints & Assumptions
- The MVP must be delivered within a 3-month timeline.
- Limited budget for extensive testing tools and resources.
- Assumption that early adopters will provide feedback for iterative improvements.

#### Critical Success Factors
- Seamless user experience with minimal bugs.
- Efficient and secure transaction processing.
- High availability and performance of the platform.
- Positive feedback from early adopters to validate the business model.

#### Time Zones
- The development team is distributed across different time zones (PST, EST, and IST). Overlapping working hours will be critical for effective communication and collaboration.
