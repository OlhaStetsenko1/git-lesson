### Risk Description

**Title:** Forking Entities Errors

**Description:** Issues with forking entities like Agents, Datasources, and Prompts across projects could disrupt collaboration efforts. This risk can lead to significant delays in project timelines, miscommunication among team members, and potential loss of data integrity. It is crucial to address this risk to ensure smooth collaboration and project continuity.

**Category:** Process

**Trigger:** Technology

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Project Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Implement regular training sessions for team members on the correct procedures for forking entities.
2. Establish a clear protocol for reporting and resolving forking issues.
3. Conduct regular audits to ensure that all entities are correctly forked and integrated.
4. Utilize automated tools to monitor and manage forking processes.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Develop and distribute comprehensive documentation on forking procedures.
2. Schedule periodic reviews of forking processes to identify and address potential issues.
3. Implement a version control system to track changes and ensure data integrity.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Have a backup system in place to restore data in case of forking errors.
2. Designate a team member to handle urgent forking issues and provide immediate support.
3. Establish a communication channel for quick resolution of forking-related problems.
