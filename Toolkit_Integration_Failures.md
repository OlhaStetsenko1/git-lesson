### Risk Description

**Title:** Toolkit Integration Failures

**Description:** New toolkits like Report Portal, TestIO, and ADO Boards may not integrate seamlessly, affecting testing and project management functionality. This could lead to delays in testing, miscommunication among team members, and potential loss of important data.

**Category:** Technology

**Trigger:** External

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Integration Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Conduct thorough compatibility testing before full-scale implementation.
2. Maintain regular communication with toolkit vendors for updates and support.
3. Develop a fallback plan in case of integration failures.
4. Provide training sessions for team members on new toolkits.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Schedule regular integration tests.
2. Allocate resources for immediate troubleshooting.
3. Establish a dedicated support team for integration issues.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Revert to previous toolkits if integration fails.
2. Use manual processes temporarily to ensure project continuity.
3. Document all issues and communicate with vendors for quick resolution.
