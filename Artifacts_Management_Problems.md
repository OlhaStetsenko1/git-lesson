### Risk Description for "Artifacts Management Problems"

1. **Title of Risk**: Artifacts Management Problems
2. **Detailed Risk Description**: Managing temporary file storage using the Artifacts Management feature may lead to organizational issues. This can result in cluttered storage, difficulty in locating necessary files, and potential data loss if files are not managed properly. The inefficiency in managing these artifacts can also lead to increased storage costs and reduced productivity as team members spend more time searching for files.
3. **Risk Category**: Process
4. **Risk Trigger**: Process and Practices
5. **Potential Value of Risk Likelihood**: Middle
6. **Potential Value of Risk Impact**: High
7. **Responsible Person**: Project Manager
8. **Treatment/Maintenance Plan**:
   - Implement a clear policy for artifact management.
   - Regularly audit and clean up temporary files.
   - Use automated tools to manage and organize artifacts.
   - Train team members on best practices for artifact management.
9. **Risk Strategy**: Mitigate
10. **Mitigation Plan Actions**:
    - Develop and enforce a standardized process for artifact management.
    - Schedule regular reviews and clean-up sessions for temporary files.
    - Utilize software tools that provide better organization and retrieval of artifacts.
11. **Contingency Plan Action List**:
    - Have a backup system in place for critical artifacts.
    - Establish a quick response team to address any immediate issues arising from artifact mismanagement.
    - Maintain a log of all artifacts and their storage locations for easy retrieval.
