### Risk Description

**Title:** Monitoring Risks

**Description:** Challenges in accurately monitoring metrics like Engagement and Acceptance Rate, especially with the introduction of the 'Monitor' role. The introduction of the 'Monitor' role may lead to inconsistencies in data collection and interpretation, resulting in unreliable metrics. This can affect decision-making processes and overall project performance.

**Category:** Quality

**Trigger:** Process and Practices

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Project Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Provide comprehensive training for the 'Monitor' role.
2. Establish clear guidelines and standardized procedures for data collection and interpretation.
3. Implement regular audits and reviews of the monitoring process.
4. Utilize automated tools to assist in data collection and analysis.
5. Encourage open communication and feedback from the 'Monitor' role to identify and address issues promptly.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Develop and deliver training sessions for the 'Monitor' role.
2. Create and distribute detailed guidelines and procedures for monitoring metrics.
3. Schedule regular audits and reviews to ensure adherence to standards.
4. Integrate automated tools to support the monitoring process.
5. Set up a feedback mechanism for continuous improvement.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Reassign monitoring responsibilities to experienced team members if issues persist.
2. Adjust project timelines to accommodate for potential delays in accurate data collection.
3. Increase oversight and support for the 'Monitor' role during the initial implementation phase.
