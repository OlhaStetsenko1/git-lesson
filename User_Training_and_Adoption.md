### Risk Description

**Title:** User Training and Adoption

**Description:** There is a risk that users may face difficulties in adapting to new features and enhancements introduced in the product. This could lead to a need for additional training sessions, which may incur extra costs and time. The lack of proper adoption could also result in decreased productivity and user satisfaction.

**Category:** Team-Performance

**Trigger:** Communication

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Training Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Conduct a thorough needs assessment to identify potential training requirements.
2. Develop comprehensive training materials and resources.
3. Schedule regular training sessions and workshops.
4. Provide continuous support and feedback channels for users.
5. Monitor user adoption and gather feedback for improvements.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Create detailed user manuals and video tutorials.
2. Implement a phased rollout of new features to allow gradual adaptation.
3. Offer one-on-one training sessions for users who need additional help.
4. Set up a dedicated support team to address user queries promptly.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Allocate additional budget for extended training programs.
2. Engage external training consultants if internal resources are insufficient.
3. Develop a fallback plan to revert to previous versions if adoption issues are critical.
