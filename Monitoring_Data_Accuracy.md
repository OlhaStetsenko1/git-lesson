### Risk Description

**Title:** Monitoring Data Accuracy

**Description:** The new monitoring enhancements and metrics (Engagement and Acceptance Rate) might produce inaccurate data, leading to incorrect insights and decisions. This risk affects features related to monitoring enhancements and metrics for engagement and acceptance rate. The functional areas impacted include monitoring and analytics.

**Category:** Quality

**Trigger:** Technology

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Data Analytics Lead

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Implement rigorous testing procedures for new monitoring enhancements.
2. Regularly validate data accuracy through cross-verification with other data sources.
3. Conduct periodic audits of the monitoring system.
4. Provide training for team members on the importance of data accuracy and methods to ensure it.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Develop and execute a comprehensive testing plan for monitoring enhancements.
2. Establish a protocol for regular data validation and cross-checking.
3. Schedule periodic system audits to identify and rectify inaccuracies.
4. Conduct training sessions for the team on data accuracy best practices.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Have a rollback plan in place to revert to previous monitoring systems if inaccuracies are detected.
2. Prepare a communication plan to inform stakeholders about potential data inaccuracies and corrective measures.
3. Allocate resources for immediate troubleshooting and resolution of data issues.
