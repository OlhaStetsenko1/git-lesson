### Risk Description

**Title:** User Training Risks

**Description:** There is a need for adequate user training to effectively utilize new features and enhancements. This training process may be time-consuming and could potentially delay the overall project timeline if not managed properly. Additionally, insufficient training may lead to improper use of the new features, resulting in decreased productivity and user dissatisfaction.

**Category:** Team-Performance

**Trigger:** Process and Practices

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Training Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Develop a comprehensive training plan and schedule.
2. Allocate sufficient resources for training sessions.
3. Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of the training.
4. Provide continuous support and resources for users post-training.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Schedule training sessions well in advance to ensure all users can attend.
2. Use a variety of training methods (e.g., hands-on, video tutorials, documentation) to cater to different learning styles.
3. Conduct regular feedback sessions to identify and address any training gaps.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Have a backup team of trainers available to handle any unexpected increase in training needs.
2. Implement a phased rollout of new features to allow for gradual adaptation and training.
3. Provide additional training sessions if initial training proves insufficient.
