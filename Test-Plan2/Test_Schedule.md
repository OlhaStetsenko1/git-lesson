### Test Schedule

The following section outlines the timeline for the testing activities for the mobile banking application project, including key milestones and deadlines. This ensures that all testing activities are completed on time and align with the overall project schedule.

| **Activity**                  | **Start Date**  | **End Date**    | **Description**                                                                                       |
|-------------------------------|-----------------|-----------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Test Planning**             | Nov 15, 2024    | Dec 14, 2024    | * Develop the test plan and strategy. <br> * Identify test resources and prepare the test environment. <br> * Review and approve the test plan. |
| **Test Design**               | Dec 15, 2024    | Jan 14, 2025    | * Create and review test cases and test scripts. <br> * Prepare test data. <br> * Set up test management tools. |
| **Test Execution**            | Jan 15, 2025    | Mar 14, 2025    | * Execute test cases and report defects. <br> * Perform functional, regression, and system testing. <br> * Conduct performance and security testing. |
| **Defect Fixing and Retesting**| Mar 15, 2025    | Apr 14, 2025    | * Fix reported defects and perform retesting. <br> * Conduct regression testing to ensure defect fixes do not introduce new issues. |
| **Test Closure**              | Apr 15, 2025    | Apr 30, 2025    | * Prepare test summary report. <br> * Conduct test closure meeting and review. <br> * Archive test artifacts and documentation. |

#### Key Milestones
* **Test Plan Approval:** Dec 14, 2024
* **Test Case Review Completion:** Jan 14, 2025
* **Test Execution Start:** Jan 15, 2025
* **Test Execution Completion:** Mar 14, 2025
* **Defect Fixing and Retesting Completion:** Apr 14, 2025
* **Test Closure:** Apr 30, 2025

#### Notes
* The test schedule is subject to change based on project progress and any unforeseen issues.
* Regular status meetings will be held to track progress and address any delays or challenges.
* All test activities will be documented and tracked using the test management tool.
