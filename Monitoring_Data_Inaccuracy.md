### Risk Description

**Title:** Monitoring Data Inaccuracy

**Description:** There is a risk that enhancements made to the monitoring system may provide inaccurate metrics. This could lead to incorrect insights being drawn from the data, potentially causing misguided decisions and actions.

**Category:** Quality

**Trigger:** Technology

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Data Quality Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Implement rigorous testing procedures for monitoring enhancements.
2. Conduct regular audits of the monitoring system.
3. Provide training for staff on the importance of data accuracy.
4. Establish a feedback loop for continuous improvement.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Develop and execute a comprehensive testing plan for all monitoring enhancements.
2. Schedule periodic reviews and audits of the monitoring data.
3. Train team members on best practices for ensuring data accuracy.
4. Create a protocol for quickly addressing any identified inaccuracies.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Roll back to the previous version of the monitoring system if inaccuracies are detected.
2. Engage a third-party expert to review and validate the monitoring data.
3. Communicate any identified issues and their impact to stakeholders promptly.
4. Develop a corrective action plan to address and rectify the inaccuracies.
