**1. PROJECT SPECIFIC IMPACT TO TESTING**

**Project Phase:**
The application is currently in the development phase. This means that the testing process will need to be closely integrated with the development activities to ensure that any issues are identified and resolved promptly.

**Technology Stack:**
The project utilizes a technology stack that includes JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB. Testing will need to cover the compatibility and integration of these technologies, as well as performance and security aspects.

**Constraints and Assumptions:**
- Limited resources: The testing team has a limited number of testers, which may impact the thoroughness and speed of testing.
- Specific deadlines: The project has strict deadlines for the release of core functionalities, which may require prioritizing certain tests over others.
- Assumption: The development team will provide timely fixes for any issues identified during testing.

**Critical Success Factors:**
- Effective communication and collaboration between the distributed project team members.
- Timely identification and resolution of critical issues.
- Comprehensive test coverage of core functionalities to ensure a stable release.

**Project Geography:**
The project team is distributed across different time zones, including the US, Europe, and Asia. This may impact the coordination of testing activities and require flexible working hours for the testing team.
