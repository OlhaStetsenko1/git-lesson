### Risk Description

**Title:** Monitoring Metrics Inaccuracy

**Description:** Metrics such as Engagement and Acceptance Rate may not accurately reflect user interactions. This can lead to incorrect insights, which may affect decision-making processes and overall project outcomes.

**Category:** Quality

**Trigger:** Process and Practices

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Data Analyst Lead

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Regularly review and validate metrics.
2. Implement cross-verification with alternative data sources.
3. Conduct periodic audits of data collection processes.
4. Train team members on accurate data interpretation.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Develop a robust validation framework for metrics.
2. Use multiple data sources for cross-verification.
3. Schedule regular audits and reviews of data collection and analysis processes.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Establish a rapid response team to address any identified inaccuracies.
2. Develop a communication plan to inform stakeholders of any issues and corrective actions.
3. Implement temporary manual checks if automated systems fail.
