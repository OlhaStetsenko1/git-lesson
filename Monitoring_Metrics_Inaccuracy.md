### Risk Description

**Title:** Monitoring Metrics Inaccuracy

**Description:** Inaccurate monitoring metrics could lead to incorrect assessments of system performance. This risk arises when the metrics used to monitor system performance are not reliable or accurate, leading to potential misinterpretations and misguided decisions. This can affect the overall quality and reliability of the system, potentially causing delays and increased costs.

**Category:** Quality

**Trigger:** Technology

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** System Performance Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Regularly calibrate and validate monitoring tools.
2. Implement redundancy in monitoring systems to cross-verify data.
3. Conduct periodic reviews and audits of monitoring metrics.
4. Train team members on the importance of accurate data collection and interpretation.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Establish a routine schedule for calibration and validation of monitoring tools.
2. Use multiple monitoring tools to cross-check and verify the accuracy of metrics.
3. Perform regular audits and reviews of the collected data to ensure accuracy.
4. Provide training sessions for the team on best practices for data collection and interpretation.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Develop a protocol for immediate investigation and correction of identified inaccuracies.
2. Maintain a backup system for critical monitoring tools.
3. Have a predefined process for manual verification of metrics in case of tool failure.
4. Prepare a communication plan to inform stakeholders about potential inaccuracies and corrective actions.
