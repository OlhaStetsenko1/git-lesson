### Risk Management

This section identifies potential risks to the testing process and describes how these risks will be managed for the e-commerce platform MVP.

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation Strategy | Monitoring |
|------|------------|--------|---------------------|------------|
| Delays in development affecting the testing schedule | High | High | Regular progress reviews and adjustments to the schedule as needed | Regularly review and update the risk management plan |
| Insufficient test data leading to incomplete test coverage | Medium | Medium | Prepare comprehensive test data sets in advance and validate them | Conduct risk assessment meetings with the project team |
| Limited availability of testing resources | Medium | High | Allocate additional resources and cross-train team members | Monitor the status of identified risks and mitigation actions |
| Integration issues with third-party services (e.g., Stripe) | Medium | High | Conduct early integration testing and maintain close communication with third-party providers | Document and communicate any new risks that arise during the project |
| Security vulnerabilities in the application | Low | High | Perform thorough security testing and code reviews | Adjust the test plan and schedule as needed based on risk status |
| Performance issues under high user load | Medium | High | Conduct performance testing and optimize the application for scalability | Regularly review and update the risk management plan |
| Inadequate test environment setup | Low | Medium | Ensure the test environment mirrors the production environment and is set up early in the project | Conduct risk assessment meetings with the project team |
