### Risk Description

**Title:** Icon Customization Issues

**Detailed Risk Description:** There is a risk that issues with pre-existing or custom icons may affect the organization of entities within the system. This could lead to confusion and inefficiency in the use of the system, as users may struggle to identify and differentiate between various entities due to icon-related problems.

**Category:** Quality

**Trigger:** Technology

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Product Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Conduct thorough testing of icon customization features.
2. Implement user feedback mechanisms to identify icon-related issues early.
3. Ensure robust documentation and guidelines for icon customization.
4. Regularly update and maintain icon libraries to prevent outdated or incompatible icons.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Develop automated tests to verify icon functionality.
2. Schedule regular reviews and updates of icon libraries.
3. Provide training sessions for users on effective icon customization practices.
4. Establish a dedicated support channel for icon-related issues.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Prepare alternative icon sets that can be quickly deployed if issues arise.
2. Develop a rollback plan to revert to default icons in case of severe problems.
3. Maintain a list of known issues and workarounds to assist users in troubleshooting.
