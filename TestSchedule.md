### Test Schedule

This section outlines the schedule for testing activities for the e-commerce platform MVP.

#### Key Milestones
- **Project Kickoff**: December 1, 2024
- **Test Planning Completion**: December 15, 2024
- **Test Case Design Completion**: January 15, 2025
- **Unit Testing Start**: January 16, 2025
- **Unit Testing Completion**: January 31, 2025
- **Integration Testing Start**: February 1, 2025
- **Integration Testing Completion**: February 15, 2025
- **System Testing Start**: February 16, 2025
- **System Testing Completion**: March 15, 2025
- **Acceptance Testing Start**: March 16, 2025
- **Acceptance Testing Completion**: March 31, 2025
- **Final Test Report**: April 5, 2025
- **Project Go-Live**: April 15, 2025

#### Test Phases

| Test Phase | Start Date | End Date | Activities |
|------------|------------|----------|------------|
| Test Planning | December 1, 2024 | December 15, 2024 | Develop the test plan and strategy. Allocate resources and assign responsibilities. Prepare the test environment and test data. |
| Test Case Design | December 16, 2024 | January 15, 2025 | Analyze requirements and design test cases. Review and validate test cases with stakeholders. Prepare test data and set up the test environment. |
| Unit Testing | January 16, 2025 | January 31, 2025 | Execute unit tests for individual components. Record test results and log defects. Perform re-testing after defect fixes. |
| Integration Testing | February 1, 2025 | February 15, 2025 | Test interactions between integrated components. Record test results and log defects. Perform re-testing after defect fixes. |
| System Testing | February 16, 2025 | March 15, 2025 | Test the complete and integrated software system. Perform functional, performance, and security testing. Record test results and log defects. Perform re-testing after defect fixes. |
| Acceptance Testing | March 16, 2025 | March 31, 2025 | Test the system against user requirements and business needs. Record test results and log defects. Perform re-testing after defect fixes. |
| Test Reporting and Closure | April 1, 2025 | April 5, 2025 | Generate the final test report. Conduct test closure meetings. Archive test artifacts for future reference. |

#### Dependencies
- Availability of development resources for fixing defects.
- Timely setup of the test environment.
- Availability of test data.
- Coordination with third-party service providers (e.g., Stripe).
- Timely completion of development milestones.
