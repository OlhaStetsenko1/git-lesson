### Risk Description

**Title:** Toolkit Test Connection Problems

**Description:** There is a risk that toolkits may fail to connect during testing, which can significantly affect the reliability and accuracy of the testing process. This could lead to delays in the project schedule, increased costs, and potential quality issues in the final product.

**Category:** Technology

**Trigger:** Infrastructure

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** Test Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Ensure all toolkits are updated to the latest versions.
2. Conduct regular connectivity tests before the main testing phase.
3. Maintain a backup toolkit to switch to in case of connection failures.
4. Provide training to the team on troubleshooting common connection issues.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Schedule regular updates and maintenance for all toolkits.
2. Implement a monitoring system to detect connection issues early.
3. Develop a troubleshooting guide for quick resolution of common issues.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Have a backup toolkit ready for immediate use.
2. Allocate additional time in the project schedule to account for potential delays.
3. Prepare a manual testing process as a last resort.
