### Risk Description

**Title:** VPN Access Dependency

**Description:** There is a risk that users will need VPN access to use the Nexus Elitea Environment. This dependency can affect seamless collaboration among team members, especially those working remotely or from different geographical locations. The need for VPN access can introduce delays, connectivity issues, and potential security vulnerabilities, impacting the overall efficiency and productivity of the project.

**Category:** Infrastructure

**Trigger:** Technology

**Likelihood:** Middle

**Impact:** High

**Responsible Person:** IT Manager

**Treatment/Maintenance Plan:**
1. Evaluate the necessity of VPN access for all users.
2. Implement alternative secure access methods if possible.
3. Ensure robust VPN infrastructure to handle the load.
4. Provide training and support for users on VPN usage.
5. Regularly monitor and maintain VPN performance and security.

**Risk Strategy:** Mitigate

**Mitigation Plan Actions:**
1. Assess and implement alternative secure access solutions.
2. Upgrade VPN infrastructure to support increased usage.
3. Conduct regular training sessions for users on VPN best practices.
4. Monitor VPN performance and address issues promptly.

**Contingency Plan Actions:**
1. Establish a backup access method in case of VPN failure.
2. Develop a quick-response team to address VPN-related issues.
3. Communicate alternative collaboration tools to users in case of VPN downtime.
