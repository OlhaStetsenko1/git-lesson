### Test Team

The following section defines the roles and responsibilities of the test team members for the mobile banking application project. This ensures a clear understanding of who will be involved in the testing process and their specific duties.

| **Role**                     | **Name**           | **Responsibilities**                                                                                       |
|------------------------------|--------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Test Manager**             | John Doe           | * Oversee the entire testing process and ensure alignment with project goals. <br> * Develop and maintain the test plan. <br> * Allocate resources and manage the test schedule. <br> * Communicate with stakeholders and provide regular status updates. |
| **Test Lead**                | Jane Smith         | * Lead the test team and coordinate testing activities. <br> * Review and approve test cases and test scripts. <br> * Monitor test execution and ensure adherence to the test plan. <br> * Identify and mitigate risks related to testing. |
| **Test Engineers**           | Alice Johnson, Bob Williams, Carol Martinez | * Design and execute test cases based on requirements and specifications. <br> * Report and track defects using the defect tracking tool. <br> * Perform functional, regression, and system testing. <br> * Collaborate with developers to resolve issues. |
| **Automation Test Engineers**| David Brown, Emily Davis | * Develop and maintain automated test scripts. <br> * Execute automated tests and analyze results. <br> * Integrate automated tests into the CI/CD pipeline. <br> * Identify opportunities for test automation and improve test coverage. |

#### Additional Responsibilities
* **Test Manager:**
  * Ensure the test environment is set up and maintained.
  * Conduct regular test progress meetings and reviews.
  * Ensure compliance with testing standards and best practices.
* **Test Lead:**
  * Provide guidance and support to test engineers.
  * Ensure test data is prepared and available for testing.
  * Review test reports and metrics to assess test progress and quality.
* **Test Engineers:**
  * Create and maintain test documentation, including test cases, test scripts, and test data.
  * Perform exploratory testing to identify potential issues.
  * Participate in test planning and estimation activities.
* **Automation Test Engineers:**
  * Collaborate with test engineers to identify test cases for automation.
  * Maintain and update the test automation framework.
  * Provide training and support to the test team on automation tools and practices.
